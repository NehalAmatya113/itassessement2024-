About WatchX

WatchX is an integrated and independent Swiss watch manufacturer headquartered in Geneva, renowned for its unparalleled expertise and commitment to quality.

Founded by Hans Wilsdorf, WatchX is synonymous with innovation, introducing groundbreaking technologies such as the Oyster, the world's first waterproof wristwatch, and the Perpetual rotor self-winding mechanism.

We take pride in designing, developing, and crafting the majority of our watch components in-house, ensuring uncompromising quality and precision. At WatchX, we not only create exceptional timepieces but also advocate for the arts, culture, and environmental sustainability.

Driven by a vision of perpetual excellence, WatchX strives to elevate not only the standards of watchmaking but also its impact on society and the environment, making each day count.
